The parrot

Parrots can be found in: Australia and Oceania, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Africa, Central and South America. They are colorful birds with massive and hooked tip. Their diet consist of: fruits, seeds, nectar, pollen, buds, arthropods, animal prey. Their nests are in hollows.

  • Number of species: 356
  • Max weight: 4kg
  • Max length: 1m
  • Max life's lenght: 100 years

Smart and easy going. Colorful and loud. Love to learn and climb.
